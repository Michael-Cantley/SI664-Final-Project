About Video Games Sales

This site draws from the Kaggle Video Games Data. The data presented here will be the Sales Data/records for a given video game for up to all five listed regions: Global, European Union, Japan, North America and Other. Video Games can be searched manually through the list view under "Games" or can be searched using a filter under "Search".The "Developers" will provide all video_games created by a developer and allow for modifications by authenticated users. Lastly, the sale ID and millions of units sold can be found for each video game with "Sales" data available.
